#### which should I use? `component` vs. `directive`

it depends

When you use a **component**, you *create* DOM, when you use .directive() you decorate DOM, that is the mindset A **directive** *decorates* the DOM, it adds behaviour and extends existing DOM.
**Components** are restricted to 'E' and cannot be changed otherwise. **Directives** *should* be restricted to 'A', as they are intended to decorate.
